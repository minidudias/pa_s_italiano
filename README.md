Pa's Italiano is a comprehensive web-based Italian cuisine delivery service designed to provide a broad selection of traditional meals at exceptional prices. The platform features a robust customer interface that allows users to securely create accounts, recover passwords via email verification , and browse products through advanced search options filtering by category, price, and series. Customers can manage their shopping experience with a dynamic cart , save items to a watchlist , review past purchases in the "Recent Buyings" section to leave feedback , and communicate directly with support via an integrated chat system , all while receiving detailed printable invoices for every transaction. On the administrative side, the system offers a secure, code-verified login leading to a dashboard that tracks daily and monthly earnings, sales metrics, and active users , empowering business owners to fully manage the inventory by adding new listings or updating existing product details and images. Furthermore, administrators can oversee the entire order lifecycle—from confirmation to packing, dispatch, and shipping —and manage user accounts by blocking restricted users or responding to customer inquiries , making this a complete e-commerce solution developed by Dodampe Gamage Minindu Dias utilizing PHP and SQL technologies.
